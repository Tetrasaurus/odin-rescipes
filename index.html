Unqualified Jumper?

The first step before following your respective dreams of flying/freefall is to become a qualifed skydiver. There are many freefall associations throughout the world with similar regulations that cover the most important aspects of your own safety, ranging from emergency procedures, equipment understanding/uses, aircraft rules, ground rules, freefall procedures and canopy flight basics. After a specified number of different completed skydivers you will be signed off to jump as a solo skydiver, you will be responsible for yourself and your own safety(with limited supervision) - You cannot begin to start any of the further disciplines until you are able to look after yourself as a qualified member of a respective association.
